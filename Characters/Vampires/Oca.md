# Character Inspiration and Development

I was facing oral surgery and it was incredibly stressful, so stressful that I just started imagining being on ship on it's way to Venice 
a bit before Armand was taken. Oca is a self-insert and although their alters aren't my alters, I wanted to show how me and my alters 
interact with each other. I wanted a little D.I.D representation in a more positive light.
