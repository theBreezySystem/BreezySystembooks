# Character Inspiration and Development

I learned that "ancient merchant ships had a supercargo." So I looked up that role and wanted to add one. <br>
I wanted him to be well-dressed and found out a lot about how fabric was dyed black in the 1400's.

## Clothing

Lorenzo wears a black chaperone hat, a stiff white collar. His hose has one eartly red tone leg and one darker blue leg. <br>
He has a dark black gown and a fur overgown. 

"The tall one had clothes like the darkest of night skies! <br>
His night sky hat was round all over. He also had something pale, sticking up by his neck, <br>
but it seemed stiffer than the one at Marco’s neck. It seemed paler as well. <br>
The blanket that covered his body was also night colored. <br>
It clung strangely to his arms like the creature’s new clothes did. <br>
He had a huge hide blanket around everything, his night covered arms coming out of the hide. <br>
His legs were bright like fish! Different colored fish! Each leg had a different color!<br>
His sandals were closed as well, but didn’t crawl up his bright legs."
