# Character Inspiration and Development

Matteo was supposed to be a giant, loveable teddy bear for everyone to fall in love with so I could
kill him off and it would be absolutely tragic. I gave him a wife and kids, and a mulberry bush and
a fig tree that his grandfather planted in his childhood home. I really wanted his death to be
impactfull, but then I liked him too much, and realized I was making a fanfic to avoid death. So, I 
decided to keep him alive forever after that.

## Birth and Early Life

<p>Born in Chioggia to a fisherman and his wife, Matteo spent a large amount of time on the water as a kid. <br>
His paternal grandmother, Nonna Cat, raised silkworms on their mulberry bushes and taught Matteo and his sister <br>
about caring for the plants and silkworms.<br>
Nonna Cat died when he was 4 and his father died a few years after that, leaving Matteo as the "man of the house," at a young age.<br></p>

<p>Matteo went and worked on merchant ships, eventually meeting Marco. <br>
